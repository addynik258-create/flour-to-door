About Our Service

Flour to Door ensures that every household gets access to fresh, natural, and chemical-free wheat flour. We begin from the roots — literally. Our wheat is cultivated under proper care using safe agricultural practices.

From soil preparation to harvesting, we maintain strict quality control. Farmers associated with us follow clean and safe farming techniques, ensuring the wheat retains its natural nutrients.

Once harvested, grains are cleaned, stored, and processed hygienically. No harmful chemicals or preservatives are used at any stage. We remove middlemen and directly connect farmers to consumers, ensuring fair pricing.
